Sunday does the boring parts — portioned beef balls, burger sauce, toppings boxed — so a weeknight double smash takes twelve minutes and zero decisions. Smash burgers are the rare meal that cannot be prepped ahead and does not need to be: the cooking is the fast part, and it is the best ten minutes the flat top sees all week. Lacy edges, cheese folded over, sauce on a toasted bun.

## Ingredients

- 3 lb | ground beef | meat | 80/20 if your Costco carries it; the leaner Kirkland works with a knob of butter on the griddle
- 6 | brioche buns | bakery | the Costco bakery pack
- 12 slices | american cheese | dairy-eggs | this is not the night for good cheddar; american melts like it loves you
- 1 head | butter lettuce | produce
- 2 | roma tomatoes | produce
- 1 | red onions | produce
- 1 jar | dill pickle chips | pantry
- 1 | salad kit | produce | the token vegetable, and a good one

### Pantry staples

- kosher salt and black pepper
- mayonnaise, ketchup, yellow mustard — plus a spoon of pickle brine and smoked paprika for the sauce
- butter, for the buns and lean-beef insurance

## Sunday

1. Portion the beef into twelve 4-ounce balls, no seasoning, no working the meat — just loose spheres on a parchment-lined container, stacked with parchment between layers.
2. Burger sauce: {110 g / 0.5 cup} mayo, {34 g / 2 tbsp} ketchup, {15 g / 1 tbsp} mustard, a spoon of pickle brine, smoked paprika, black pepper. Jar it.
3. That's it. Twenty-five minutes including cleanup. This recipe exists so one dinner each week costs Sunday almost nothing.

## Weeknight

- Flat top to maximum, a full ten minutes of preheat — the sear budget is stored in the metal.
- Slice tomato and onion. Butter and toast the buns on the cooler edge.
- Balls on, smash hard and thin with a spatula (a second spatula on top for leverage), salt generously, 90 seconds to a crusted edge. Flip and scrape — keep the crust — cheese on, 45 seconds. Stack doubles.
- Sauce both bun faces; lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles. Salad kit into a bowl. Dinner in twelve minutes.

## Notes

- The smash only works within the first 30 seconds while the meat is cold; commit.
- Kendra version: single instead of double, extra salad. The single is still a real burger.
- Day 5 check: raw beef balls should be cooked by day 3 or moved to the freezer Sunday and thawed the night before — schedule burger night accordingly. The prepped toppings and sauce keep all week.
